## Overview

LDS Database is a personal scripture reading app for Latter-day Saint texts. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

**This app collects no information whatsoever. Nothing you do in the app is transmitted anywhere.**

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, use, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information or data of any kind. Specifically:

- We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or device ID.
- We do not collect any information from your device, including location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or files.
- We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or usage tracking of any kind.
- We do not show advertisements and do not use any advertising or marketing services or networks.
- We do not send emails or any other communications.
- We do not offer in-app purchases, subscriptions, or any transactions.
- We do not use any third-party SDKs, APIs, providers, or services.
- We do not collect any information from children or adults.

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only** using iOS/Android system storage (AsyncStorage). This data is never transmitted, shared, or accessible to us or any third party:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Highlights | Character ranges you have highlighted in scripture text |
| Bookmarks | Chapters you have bookmarked |
| Font size preference | Your chosen reading text size |
| Search section preferences | Which collections are included in database-wide search |

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the app.
